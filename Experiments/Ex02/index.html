1. Ordered List

An ordered list is used for items where the sequence matters.

  1. First item
  2. Second item
  3. Third item

2. Unordered List

An unordered list is used for items where the sequence does not matter (a bulleted list).

  • Coffee
  • Tea
  • Milk

3. Definition List

A definition list is used to list terms and their definitions.
