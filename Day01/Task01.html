France

Country Details

France, officially the French Republic, is a country located primarily in Western Europe. It also includes various overseas regions and territories. Metropolitan France extends from the Mediterranean Sea to the English Channel and the North Sea, and from the Rhine to the Atlantic Ocean. It is a unitary semi-presidential republic.

Capital

The capital of France is Paris.
